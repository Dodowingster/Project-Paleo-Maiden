================================================================================
THE SPATIAL TECHNIQUE SYSTEM: KOMA GRID, MASTERY, & REPETITION DECAY
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. THE PROGRESSION LOOP ARCHITECTURE (CAREER TO GAUNTLET)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The macro loop establishes a strict, irreversible barrier between the preparation/
coaching phase and the competitive endgame arcade gauntlet.

  - THE ACQUISITION PHASE (Career Mode): Over a 3-year timeline (24 turns/year), 
    the coach balances stat training, cooking, and sparring to discover and 
    practice techniques. Learned moves populate the run's Technique Library.
  - THE ARTIFACT SNAPSHOT (Run End): Upon completing the final 3rd-Year 
    Cretaceous League, the Maiden's final stats and her surviving Technique 
    Library are permanently frozen as a "Final Build" portrait in the Gallery.
  - THE EXECUTION PHASE (Gauntlet Mode): The player fields a frozen Final Build 
    in arcade ladder runs. They can no longer learn moves or alter stats; they act 
    strictly as a "Corner Coach," re-arranging the Active Grid between match 
    rounds to counter specific opponent styles using their entire surviving vault.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. TECHNIQUE KOMA SHAPES & THE MASTERY MATRIX
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Techniques do not occupy flat text rows. Inspired by spatial inventory design, 
moves exist as physical blocks (Komas) that must be fit onto a bounded matrix board. 
Repeatedly drilling a move during the Career Run yields Mastery, physically 
compressing its block footprint due to streamlined execution frames.

  ┌──────────────────────────┬───────────┬───────────┬────────────────────────┐
  │ Technique Tier           │ Base Size │ Max Mast. │ Fighting Game Function │
  ├──────────────────────────┼───────────┼───────────┼────────────────────────┤
  │ Core Specials (236/etc)  │ 3 Slots   │ 1 Slot    │ Starters, Space Control│
  │ Ex Specials              │ 5 Slots   │ 3 Slots   │ i-frames, Extenders, EX│
  │ Uniques (Base/Inherited) │ 7 Slots   │ 5 Slots   │ Character Cinematic Ult│
  │ Vocation Uniques         │ 7 Slots   │ 5 Slots   │ Evolved Style Finisher │
  └──────────────────────────┴───────────┴───────────┴────────────────────────┘

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. THE DYNAMIC ACTIVE GRID & HYBRID EVOLUTION DISCOUNT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The Active Grid represents the Maiden's immediate equipped cognitive capacity for 
an upcoming battle. Her mind expands and optimizes as she evolves.

  - THE BASELINE CAPACITY: 10 Slots (5x2 Matrix Grid)
    All Maidens start with this restricted layout. An unoptimized Maiden trying 
    to force an unmastered Unique (7 slots) and a single unmastered Special 
    (3 slots) will entirely saturate her grid, choking her tactical options.
  
  - THE SHIFT EXPANSION: 16 Slots (4x4 Matrix Grid)
    To achieve advanced forms, players must fit precise elemental/discipline block 
    ratios (e.g., Flow + Focus) into the 10-slot grid. Success triggers a 
    Vocation Shift (e.g., Boxing Magus). The board physically fractures and 
    slides outward into a massive 4x4 grid, unlocking a Vocation Unique slot and 
    permitting high-tier "Dream Builds."
    * Note: Bulky, unmastered 3-slot blocks or unmaintained Rusty blocks will 
      physically choke the baseline grid, preventing the shift from occurring.
    
  - THE NATIVE STYLE COGNITIVE DISCOUNT:
    The moment a Maiden successfully shifts into a hybrid Vocation, her mental 
    evolution grants an automatic, permanent spatial discount to her NATIVE Unique. 
    Her base signature ultimate becomes instantly more integrated into her combat 
    subconscious, shrinking its footprint by exactly -2 slots:
      * Unmastered Native Unique: Compresses from 7 Slots -> down to 5 Slots.
      * Fully Mastered Native Unique: Compresses from 5 Slots -> down to 3 Slots.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. THE AUTOMATIC RETENTION FILTER (TIME DECAY VS. MASTERY)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To prevent absolute hoarding and force tactical player commitment during Career Mode, 
unrefined techniques decay organically if neglected. Players cannot manually choose 
to delete or "forget" techniques via menus; decay is governed strictly by time, 
deployment, and mastery thresholds.

  - THE MASTERY IMMUNITY RULE (Permanent Muscle Memory)
    High-repetition training resulting in Max Mastery represents an indelible 
    neurological shift in the Maiden's style. Once a technique achieves Max Mastery 
    (e.g., Core Specials compressed to 1 slot, Uniques compressed to 3 or 5 slots), 
    it becomes 100% IMMUNE to Rust. It is safe forever, can never expand, and can 
    never be forgotten.

  - PHASE 1: THE 3-TURN TIME WARNING (The Rust State)
    If an unmastered technique sits benched in the Library—or resides on the 
    Active Grid but fails to be deployed—for THREE consecutive Career turns, it 
    accumulates "Rust."
      * THE WARNING SIGNAL: The Koma block tarnishes visually and balloons in 
        size (+1 to +2 slots), signaling that muscle memory is degrading.
      * THE ON-GRID OVERLOAD: If a move rusts while actively equipped and its 
        expansion pushes the grid beyond its capacity limits, the board becomes 
        "Overloaded," penalizing the Maiden's starting frame data or meter.
      * THE EXPLOIT-PROOF LIFELINE (Deployment-Based): A player can remove Rust at 
        any point during the year by DEPLOYING the move—meaning they equip it to 
        the grid and complete exactly one live sparring or tournament match. They 
        do not rely on RNG AI execution; the player pays the cost purely by navigating 
        a high-stakes match with a bulky, clunky grid layout to refresh her focus.

  - PHASE 2: THE ANNUAL TRANSITION (The Forgotten Wipe)
    At the conclusion of each calendar year (Turn 24), the Maiden cleans out her 
    volatile mental clutter before advancing to the next cycle.
      * THE PURGE RULE: Any unmastered technique that is actively "Rusty" at the 
        exact moment the year rolls over is permanently scrubbed from her muscle 
        memory and disappears from the Technique Library.
      * THE SHARP IMMUNITY: Any move kept "Sharp" via active deployment rotation, 
        protected by Max Mastery, or securely maintained within a valid, 
        un-overloaded grid safely transitions into the next year.

  - THE CRETACEOUS LEAGUE EXCEPTION (The Final Rescue Window)
    The Cretaceous League at the end of Year 3 represents the final consecutive 
    match series before the permanent Gallery Lock and transition to Gauntlet Mode.
      * LIVE RESCUE MECHANIC: Because the League features a multi-battle bracket 
        format, players can utilize early tournament rounds to deliberately deploy 
        Rusty, unmastered techniques. Completing those high-stakes tournament rounds 
        clears their decay instantly, saving them from the final wipe.
      * THE GAUNTLET TRANSITION: The moment the tournament ends, remaining 
        Rusty techniques are purged. The surviving "Sharp" library is locked 
        into the Gauntlet Mode roster with all Rust mechanics entirely deactivated 
        permanently.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. ESTABLISHED HIGH-LEVEL CAREER METAGAME
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This system divides the 3-year Career progression into a highly distinct, 
satisfying tactical macro-loop:

  - YEAR 1 (The Sponging Phase): The coach hogs as many techniques as possible, 
    widely expanding her library to experiment with elemental combinations while 
    beginning to master her core foundational specials to preserve early grid space.
  - YEAR 2 (The Culling Phase): The player heavily prioritizes mastering the exact 
    techniques needed to satisfy their target Hybrid Vocation Shift. Niche, high-tier 
    moves intended for the endgame are put on a strict "maintenance deployment 
    rotation" to prevent them from being wiped out at the Year 2 rollover.
  - YEAR 3 (The Polishing Phase): The coach finishes mastering the core toolkit to 
    earn permanent Rust Immunity. With their main combat style compressed, they use 
    the final turns and early Cretaceous League bracket matches to de-rust any 
    remaining unmastered secret weapons, cementing a massive, versatile strategy 
    vault for the Gauntlet.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. ENDGAME GAUNTLET METAGAME & BALANCE REGULATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - PURPOSED DESIGN ALIGNMENT: By handling the permanent removal of moves via 
    the Career Mode's annual transitions, Gauntlet Mode does not require artificial 
    menu deletion. The player enters the endgame with a massive, permanent 
    tactical toolbox—the ultimate mechanical reward for a perfectly optimized 
    Career Run.
  
  - THE CORNER-COACH MATRIX PUZZLE: In Gauntlet Mode, the active 16-slot grid acts 
    as the "deck-thinner." Because fully mastered moves are compressed down to 1 
    and 3 slots, a perfectly optimized Maiden has a microscopic core footprint. 
    This allows players to freely adapt between ladder rounds, swapping utility, 
    armor-breaking, or zoning tools on the fly to counter hyper-threat bosses like 
    Saurophaganax without altering her core identity.
  
  - THE "DUAL-UNIQUE" SYNERGY: A player who unlocks a hybrid Vocation can achieve 
    a devastating Dual-Ultimate build. Thanks to the Native Style Cognitive 
    Discount, a fully mastered Native Unique only demands 3 slots post-evolution. 
    Combined with a fully mastered Vocation Unique (5 slots), the core ultimates 
    consume exactly 8 slots total. This leaves precisely half of the 16-slot grid 
    (8 slots) completely free for high-tier Ex Specials and utility counters.
