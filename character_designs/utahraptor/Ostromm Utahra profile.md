## Profile/Background

Ostromm Utahra is the fierce and commanding leader of the **"Cedar Mountain Group,"** a small, tight-knit group of Paleo Maidens. On the surface, Utahra projects an unshakeable, aristocratic composure. She maintains a rigid, silent gravity because she believes it's what her friends need to see, though her unnaturally still demeanor often leaves onlookers feeling deeply unsettled.

Despite her intimidating, predatory presence, she hides a surprising soft spot. Following a particularly demoralizing clash with her ideological rival, **Fragilis Allosa**—who holds a prestigious title that Utahra covets—she began to question her own strength. She was later found by a 10-year-old elementary school boy. 

The boy admired Ostromm Utahra, and seeing his hero feeling defeated, he offered earnest words of encouragement, reminding her that her strength and leadership inspire him. He explained that it's okay for two great champions to coexist. This encounter profoundly shifted her resolve, transforming her internal motivation from a ruthless quest for dominance into a deeply personal promise to her biggest fan: to earn a place of honor without needing to erase another's.

## Motivation and Goals

* **Primary Goal:** To win the PMC and define what it means to be the "Champion of Utah"—moving beyond a mere title toward becoming an unshakeable foundation for her home.
* **Internal Goal:** To win the championship as a tribute to her young fan. She wants to stand on the final stage and declare herself as a symbol of strength and perseverance, ensuring her legacy is recognized and making the boy's dream a reality.

## Trivia

* Her birthdate (February 18th) is a reference to the date in 2021 when the bill to make *Utahraptor* the Utah state dinosaur was officially signed.
* Her design's "vampiric" flair is a nod to the *Dromaeosauridae* family's reputation as "nocturnal hunters" in early paleontological theories, blended with a modern, elegant "Alucard-esque" aesthetic.
* Her rivalry with Kitadani Fukui is a thematic clash of dromaeosaurid-like dinosaurs: the largest "raptor" (Utahraptor) which commands the ground with devastating foot-claws, versus a megaraptoran (Fukuiraptor), whose power comes entirely from its hands.
