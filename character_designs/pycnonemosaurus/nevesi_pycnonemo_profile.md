## Profile/Background

Nevesi Pycnonemo is the reigning "Regal Idol" of the **Stratum Institute** and a high-profile competitor in the **Apex League (Carni Dorm)**. Born in Northern Brazil, she was a child prodigy who initially trained in traditional Capoeira. However, Nevesi’s narcissism led her to reject the "communal" and "earthy" aspects of the art. She viewed the requirement of touching the ground with one's hands as a "peasant's struggle," eventually abandoning her traditional training to create her own elite, solo-focused style.

She now embodies the persona of a theatrical conqueror—a mix of cool arrogance and operatic self-importance. She performs for the "Axé" (radiance) generated by her fans, using her fame to validate her existence. Her core conflict lies in her relationship with **Sastrei Taurus**. While Nevesi publicly belittles the "Pipsqueak" for her drab, disciplined boxing, she is secretly haunted by the fact that Taurus represents the authentic connection to the art that Nevesi discarded for the spotlight.

## Trivia

* While Taurus is inspired by pure boxers, Nevesi's flashy, kick-based fighting style is inspired by characters like **Elena (Street Fighter)**, **Eddy Gordo (Tekken)**, **Tidus (Final Fantasy X)**, and **Gohan (Dragon Ball FighterZ)**. However despite being inspired with light-weighted characters, her design is going for the pollar opposite approach as in what if lightweight in motion but heavy in impact direction.
* Her birthday, July 14th, is the real-world date her species was officially described in 2002.
* Her design is a "Heavyweight Speedster" experiment: she has the impact of a 92kg powerhouse but the explosive aerial mobility of a C. Viper-style fighter.
* She keeps a hidden stash of Brazilian snacks in her jacket pockets—one of the few things she refuses to share with fans.
