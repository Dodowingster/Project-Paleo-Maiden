# Gauntlet Ending Cards Draft

Status: Working draft for future authoring and iteration.
Scope: Arcade/Gauntlet alternative-answer endings after Saurophaganax resolution.

## Mode Flow Rule (Career Run -> Gauntlet)
- In a normal Career Run, Saurophaganax confrontation ends with loop reset and
  career closure.
- Before the reset completes, Saurophaganax taunts the Maiden/player to
  challenge her again "if you seek the truth behind this world."
- Gauntlet Mode is that rematch space: Saurophaganax expects the Maidens to
  return, not just to win, but to present an answer.
- Because Gauntlet is a truth-trial space, endings here are written as
  alternative-answer continuities rather than default Career Run outcomes.

## Card Format
- Maiden's Answer: Core philosophical response to Saurophaganax.
- Resolution: How that answer is enacted.
- Cost: Irreversible personal price.
- World Result: Concrete continuity/world-state change.
- Final Image: Last visual beat for memory retention.

---

## Sastrei Taurus - The Last Uppercut
- Maiden's Answer: If you are the cause of this loop, then I am sending you back to where you belong!
- Resolution: Sastrei rejects all sealing logic and drives Saurophaganax out of the Paleo-Maiden layer through a cycle-breaking strike.
- Cost: The paradox backlash erases Sastrei's existence from causal history.
- World Result: The time loop ends permanently. The world proceeds linearly.
- Final Image: Dawn over an empty training ground. Everyone moves forward. No one remembers why the wind feels like someone is missing. However old habits die-hard and they are faintly remembering the remnants of Sastrei Taurus.

## Tyran Rex - By My Decree
- Maiden's Answer: This world does not need your judgment. By my decree, this world stands by me!
- Resolution: Tyran asserts sovereign continuity law and defeats Saurophaganax's claim over the world's fate.
- Cost: Tyran relinquishes ordinary life and becomes permanent Sovereign-Warden.
- World Result: Saurophaganax yields jurisdiction and withdraws from the world layer. No reset occurs while Tyran's order endures.
- Final Image: Tyran seated upon a high tribunal, greatsword grounded before her, issuing one command as the sky clears.

## Nevesi Pycnonemo - Radiant Unison
- Maiden's Answer: The chant of the whole world for me, drowning out your voice, will decide this world's fate.
- Resolution: Nevesi converts worldwide cheering into a Radiant Covenant that overwhelms Saurophaganax's presence.
- Cost: Her radiance is bound to public duty forever; she cannot wield it for vanity alone.
- World Result: Collective resonance stabilizes continuity and forces Saurophaganax to retreat from the Paleo-Maiden world.
- Final Image: A dark arena lights section by section until the entire horizon chants in unison, brighter than any single star.

## Consistency Guardrails
- Every ending must be character-true and feel inevitable in hindsight.
- Every ending must include a real cost and a visible world gain.
- Saurophaganax is never a wish-granter; she is the truth test and adjudicator.
- Career Run must always hand off into Gauntlet through Saurophaganax's taunt
  invitation to return for the truth.
- Gauntlet endings are alternative continuity answers, not mandatory mainline canon.
