## 1. The Heritage of Speed and Strategy

Sastrei Taurus was born into the prestigious *Carnotaurus* lineage, the daughter of the legendary prime competitor **Abeli Taurus** (formerly Sastrei Taurus) and a human father who worked as a brilliant professor. Because Paleo-Maidens are entirely female and reproduce exclusively by marrying human males, her human heritage is completely natural to her world's biology.

From her mother, Sastrei inherited a foundation of incredible, explosive line speed. From her professor father, she inherited a sharp, tactical brilliance. However, she was born with a small P-Magic pool and low stamina, leaving her completely unsuited for the traditional, high-impact brute force style of her lineage.

## 2. The Bedtime Story & The Mother’s Real Fear

When Sastrei was a child, Abeli would pull her close to comfort her. She told Sastrei a family story from the era of her grandmother, **Cerato Taurus**. Abeli explained that during past Great Expeditions on the Primeval Continent, scouts had routinely caught glimpses of an untraceable, lightning-fast phantom presence darting through the ancient woods—an anomaly they dubbed the **"Ghost of Ceratos."** Abeli used this story to inspire her, telling Sastrei that raw brute force wasn't the only way to be a great Paleo Maiden.

As Sastrei grew older and stubbornly insisted on entering the brutal Stratum Institute, she constantly overexerted herself trying to copy a brute-force style her body couldn't sustain. Abeli didn't distance herself; instead, she was paralyzed by a deeply protective, maternal fear. Having already lost her human husband to a chrnoic disease, Abeli was terrified of watching her fragile daughter get physically shattered in the Apex League. Her anxiety was born entirely out of a desperate wish to keep the last piece of her family safe.

## 3. The Lecturer’s Blueprint: The Idealized Champion

When Sastrei pairs up with the **Lecturer (the player)** at the institute, she is at a complete competitive dead end.

Looking at her unique frame, her explosive acceleration, and her brilliant tactical mind, the Lecturer sees a striking resemblance to a legendary human boxing champion they have always idealized. Driven by this inspiration, the Lecturer introduces her to the art of **Boxing**. By combining her mother's inherited speed with her father's analytical tactical brilliance, she learns to slip punches, cut sharp angles, and maximize kinetic power. Together, they forge a flawless new elite archetype: **The Kinetic Boxer**.

## 4. The Paradox Closed: The Ghost of Ceratos

In the Gauntlet Mode continuity, Sastrei delivers a cycle-breaking strike to destroy Saurophaganax's time loop, sacrificing her own identity, name, and history to give the Lecturer a linear future. Sastrei Taurus is permanently gone from the world, erasing her presence from the collective memory of everyone except her Lecturer.

However, during her final, cataclysmic confrontation against Saurophaganax, Sastrei pushes her kinetic boxing style to its absolute zenith. Her velocity becomes so unfathomably fast that it tears at the fabric of reality, creating severe temporal ripple effects that bleed directly backward into the past of the Primeval Continent.

The mysterious, impossible speedster that her grandmother Cerato and mother Abeli witnessed decades ago wasn't an ancient ancestor. **The Ghost of Ceratos was Sastrei Taurus all along.** Her final act of defiance echoed through the timeline, generating the very legend that her mother would one day use to comfort her as a child.
