# Sastrei Taurus — Character Profile & Campaign Structure

## 1. Background & Heritage

| Attribute | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| **Species** | *Carnotaurus sastrei* (Abelisauridae lineage) |
| **Lineage** | Daughter of legendary prime competitor **Abeli Taurus** and a human professor. |
| **Inherited Traits** | Blinding line speed (Mother) $\times$ Analytical tactical brilliance (Father). |
| **Biological Flaw** | Severe low baseline stamina and a pathologically small P-Magic pool. |
| **The Core Conflict** | Her fragile frame completely rejects her family's traditional, brute-force style, leaving her at a competitive dead end before meeting the Lecturer. |

> ### 📜 The Maternal Fear & The Bedtime Story
> To comfort her as a fragile child, her mother Abeli would read her an ancient piece of folklore regarding the **"Ghost of Ceratos"**—a legendary, untraceable phantom made of emerald-green and deep-blue light that protected outcasts on the Primeval Continent. Having already lost her human husband to a chronic disease, Abeli’s overprotectiveness was born out of a desperate, maternal terror of watching her fragile daughter get physically shattered in the ruthless Apex League.

## 2. Campaign Prologue: The Sovereign Proving Ground

### The Shadow of the Alleyway
Before arriving at the Stratum Institute, the newly appointed **Lecturer (the player)** gets lost alongside **Caza Amarga**, who is offering directions. When human thugs ambush them demanding money, Caza is legally forbidden from deploying her league-registered P-Magic whips against ordinary civilians. 

Suddenly, Sastrei Taurus swoops down from a nearby rooftop. A thug pulls a firearm, but Sastrei executes a flawless, instinctive duck-under to evade the bullet trajectory before delivering a rapid succession of bare-fisted strikes that lay the ambushers flat. She disappears into the evening before names can be exchanged. 

Upon arrival, Student Council President **Tyran Rex** welcomes the Lecturer. She subtly notices the dust on the Lecturer's collar and the fresh bruising on Sastrei’s knuckles as the girls depart, but she strategically chooses to remain silent for the night.

### The Fractured Blade
The following afternoon during a facility tour, the Lecturer witnesses Sastrei engaged in a tense, one-sided weapon-manifestation spar. No matter how hard she concentrates, her attempts to conjure standard P-Magic weapons fail instantly; the constructs crack, splinter, and shatter into dust within seconds. The faculty has completely given up on her due to her low magic pool and solitary, uncooperative "lone wolf" attitude. The only person she actively warms up to is her childhood friend, Caza Amarga.

### The Threshold of Expulsion
Later that evening, the Lecturer finds Sastrei alone in the dark gym, her knuckles bleeding as she frantically tries and fails to hold a P-Magic weapon together. Spotting the Lecturer, her defense mechanisms spike, aggressively assuming they are there to mock her. 

Tyran Rex interrupts the interaction to formally investigate Sastrei for illegal magic usage against humans. Sastrei furiously explains she used pure physical force to protect Caza. The Lecturer steps forward, offering recorded device footage and an eyewitness testimony that immediately clears Sastrei's name. However, Tyran drops a heavy truth: 
> *"Your legal record is clean, Taurus, but your academic standings are quite lackluster. If you fail tomorrow's debut sparring match, the board will unfortunately expel you from the institute."*

### The Blueprint & The Flash Uppercut
Left alone, Sastrei reaches her boiling point and **crashes out** in absolute, quiet frustration, slamming her bare fists directly into a concrete training pillar. She has given everything to copy her lineage's brute-force methods, and her body is rejecting it. 

The Lecturer steps forward, noting that her magic isn't weak—it is simply built entirely for explosive, short-duration bursts. They offer a paradigm-shifting riddle: 
> *"Stop trying to forge a sword. What if you compressed that entire explosion into a fraction of a second, concentrated into a single square inch, right at the moment of impact?"*

Sastrei snaps that they are making zero sense and storms out. 

The next day, Sastrei stands in the ring for her debut match against the reigning Regal Idol, **Nevesi Pycnonemo**, who draws a massive crowd and openly ridicules Sastrei as a "drab pipsqueak." Sastrei struggles heavily against Nevesi's explosive leg power. Cornered against the ropes, Sastrei looks across the arena, locks eyes with the Lecturer, and finally understands the advice. 

She abandons weapon conjuration entirely. She channels her entire P-Magic pool down her arm, keeping it completely dormant until her fist is mere millimeters from Nevesi's jaw. **BOOM.** A localized, blinding kinetic detonation occurs precisely upon contact. The sheer velocity launches the 92kg powerhouse clean into the air for an immediate, jaw-dropping ring-out victory.

### "I Am All Ears, Teach"
Later that evening, Sastrei shyly approaches the Lecturer in the courtyard to thank them for the advice, the legal defense, and the footage. Stepping out of her comfort zone, she asks the Lecturer to officially team up as her designated trainer for the World Cup circuit. 

The Lecturer smiles and accepts, but notes: *"Your raw energy is incredible, but your style lacks refinement. It's too brute."* 
Sastrei crosses her arms defensively: *"Hey, cut me some slack! I literally just figured out how to use my hands properly today!"* 

The Lecturer steps closer, throwing a light, textbook shadow-boxing jab into the air. *"Then let me introduce you to a human discipline called **Boxing**. No wasted weapons. Just angles, slips, and kinetic impact."* Sastrei looks at the stance, a fierce, confident grin spreading across her face. 
> *"Fair enough. I'm all ears, Teach."*

## 4. MAX Affinity Camp Event: "The Ghost of Ceratos"

**Prerequisite:** Max Affinity reached with the Lecturer prior to the Cretaceous Grand Finals.

* **The Atmosphere**: Late evening at the training camp. Sastrei sits quietly on the edge of the ring, methodically wrapping her hands in boxing tape. Her defensive, solitary shell is completely gone.
* **The Dialogue**: She looks at her wrapped hands and quietly shares a secret she has never even told Caza:
    > *"Hey, Teach... Can I tell you something stupid?"*

    She explains that when she was a little kid, her body couldn't handle her family's brutal magic. She would lock herself away, crying because she felt like an embarrassment to the Abeli name. To comfort her, her mother Abeli would read her an ancient, forbidden piece of folklore from a dusty tome: the legend of the Ghost of Ceratos.  

    According to the fable, it was a wandering phantom made of emerald-green and deep-blue light that appeared during historical tragedies to protect outcasts and shatter the oppressive power of tyrannical monarchs.  

    Sastrei gives a soft, almost wistful chuckle:
    > *"I used to stay up late, staring out my window, praying that the Ghost would tear through the sky and save me from my own broken body. Silly, right? But then I met you. You showed me how to stop trying to force myself into a mold that wasn't mine. You gave me these fists."*

* **The Temporal Anomaly**: As she offers a soft smile and holds her fists out to display her steady posture, her P-Magic naturally flares in a comfortable, peak-affinity resonance. The Lecturer watches in shock as her localized emerald and blue aura subtly warps the air, creating a faint, microsecond-delayed visual echo of a larger, armored primeval silhouette right behind her. Sastrei, completely blind to the anomaly, simply smiles: 
    > *"I don't need to pray for a ghost to save me anymore. Because right here, on this path we built together... I'm finally strong enough to stand in my own light."*

## 5. The Paradox Closed: True Endgame Continuity

In the **Gauntlet Mode True Ending**, Sastrei is pushed to the absolute brink of deletion by Saurophaganax Maximus. Refusing to let the Lecturer suffer again, she sacrifices her own identity, name, and history to give the Lecturer a linear future, permanently vanishing from the collective memory of the world.

However, during this final, cataclysmic strike, Sastrei pushes her **Boxing Magus** style past the absolute boundaries of physical law. Her velocity becomes so unfathomably fast that it tears a rift through the time stream, bleeding her consciousness and energy directly backward into the past of the Primeval Continent.

The mysterious, impossible speedster that her grandmother Cerato and mother Abeli witnessed decades ago wasn't an ancient ancestor. **The Ghost of Ceratos was Sastrei Taurus all along.** Her final act of defiance echoed through the timeline, generating the very legend that her mother would one day use to comfort her as a child—closing the paradox forever.
