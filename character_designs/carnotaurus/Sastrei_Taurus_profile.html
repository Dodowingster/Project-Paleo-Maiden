Profile/Background

Sastrei Taurus is a Paleo-Maiden affiliated with the Apex League (Carni Dorm), but she is an ideological rebel within it. Unlike her peers, she was born with a less abundant P-Magic pool and lower innate Stamina. This forced her to reject the Carni philosophy of overwhelming Brute Force. Instead, she committed to mastering Agility and Precision, which are the core traits of the rival Bulwark League (Herbi Dorm)'s philosophy. She is a dedicated Lone Wolf who trains obsessively to prove that technique can surpass inherited power.

Fighting Style

Motivation and Goals

  • Primary Goal: To win the Paleo-Maiden Championship (PMC) and secure the most prestigious post-graduation contract.
  • Internal Goal: To achieve a style so flawless that she is never forced to rely on a large P-Magic pool for survival.

Trivia

  • Her combat heels force her into an aggressive, forward-leaning stance, embodying the Theropod's digitigrade gait and maximizing the momentum of her strikes.
  • Her childhood friendship with Caza Amarga (Amargasaurus) is a nod to real-life paleontology; the fossil remains of both the Carnotaurus and the Amargasaurus were found during the same expedition in Argentina, symbolizing their shared origins.
  • Her birthdate (27th April) is a direct reference to when her irl counterpart was found which is the year 1984. The day (27) is a numerical reference to the 1984 discovery year (19 + 8 = 27) of her real-life counterpart.
  • A lot of her moveset, animations, fighting style and design are inspired by fighting game characters that belong in the same archetype: boxing. Which are Ed from Street Fighter, Akihiko Sanada from Persona 4 Arena Ultimax and Makoto Nanaya from Blazblue.
  • The fierce Family Rivalry with Nevesi Pycnonemo (Pycnonemosaurus) is rooted in paleogeography. Pycnonemosaurus (found in the northern region of South America) is a potentially larger Abelisaurid than Carnotaurus (found in the south), creating a lore conflict between Sastrei's Agility and Nevesi's inherited Brute Force/Size within their own family line.
